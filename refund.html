Refund Policy

Know about our refund policies and conditions.

Overview

At ViMeet, we aim to provide our customers with the best experience. This refund policy explains the terms and conditions under which refunds are processed.

Eligibility for Refunds

  • Refunds are applicable only for eligible products or services purchased directly through our platform.
  • Refund requests must be made within 7 days of the purchase date.
  • Proof of purchase is required to initiate the refund process.

Non-Refundable Items

The following items are not eligible for refunds:

  • Services or products that have already been utilized.
  • Promotional items or discounts.
  • Subscription-based services after the trial period.

Refund Process

To request a refund, follow these steps:

  1. Contact our customer support at support@vimeet.com.
  2. Provide your order details, including the purchase date and transaction ID.
  3. Our team will review your request and respond within 3-5 business days.

Refund Timeline

Approved refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days, depending on your payment method and bank.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this refund policy, please contact us at support@vimeet.com.
